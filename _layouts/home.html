Aims of the workshop

Learn how to use the MHASpread v.0.2.0 package, introduction, and control of FMD

  1. Overview of the model outputs and their interpretation.
  2. MHASpread to simulate FMD countermeasure actions (depopulation, vaccination, and traceability).
  3. To be exposed to additional transmission models.
computer

Computer requirements

General, any computer medium to high quality released during the last five years should be able to support R and Rstudio, however, there are minimum computer requirements that should be considered:

  1. Intel Core i5 (4 CPUs) 6th generation or above or equivalent AMD X8 @ 4GHZ (8 CPUs) or above.
  2. Memory: 8GB.
  3. HDD Space: 65GB.
